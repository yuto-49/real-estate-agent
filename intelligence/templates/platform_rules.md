# Platform Rules

## Guardrails
- Maximum auto-approved deal value: $2,000,000 (escalate to human above this)
- Minimum offer: 50% of asking price
- Maximum negotiation rounds: 10 (force broker mediation after)
- Required disclosures: known defects, flood zone, HOA fees, lead paint, environmental hazards
- Price per sqft cap: $5,000 (flag for manual review above this)

## Negotiation Flow
Offers flow through: IDLE → OFFER_PENDING → ACCEPTED/REJECTED/COUNTER_PENDING → CONTRACT_PHASE → INSPECTION → CLOSING → CLOSED

## Agent Roles
- Buyer Agent: represents the buyer's interests exclusively
- Seller Agent: represents the seller's interests exclusively
- Broker Agent: neutral mediator ensuring fair dealing and compliance
